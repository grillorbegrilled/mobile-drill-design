To add a block of marchers to the field, click the Marchers menu and then Add Marchers.

You can resize the block, move it, and set the initial direction and interval. Click the green checkmark when you're done setting them up.

You can select marchers by clicking and dragging. Selecting marchers a second time unselects them. You can also single click on a marcher to select or deselect them.

To select a non-rectangular area, double-click on the field to start, then single-click as many times as necessary to draw the desired shape. Double-click to finish.

Use the Add Steps menu to make marchers change direction and perform other actions. Only the selected marchers will be affected. Remember, a marcher will keep performaing their current action until you tell them to do something different.

You can remove steps by using the Delete button.

Use the Paths menu to draw a path for marchers to follow. Select a direction to turn, move the mouse in front of a leader, and click on the field where you'd like them to turn. Then place the next turn and so on. You can click a turn marker to select it, drag it with the mouse to move it, or press the delete key to remove it.

Click the green check mark to save the path.
